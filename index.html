How We Trade (Simple Pipeline)

From preparation to execution—clear steps you can repeat daily.

1) Prepare

Map S/D, S/R, ORB on HTF. Note news & sessions.

2) Read

Swings, BOS/CHoCH, FVG, balance. Shift in control.

3) Confirm

Context → signal → invalidation. If clean: execute.

4) Review

Journal, note mistakes, adjust plan. Compound process.
