The FusionTicks Approach

Strategies alone are a 50/50 gamble. We flip the odds by reading the market, identifying who’s in control (buyers vs sellers), and stacking confirmations before any entry.

Read, Don’t Guess

We use core price concepts to understand control and intent—candles, swings, BOS/CHoCH, FVG, balance/imbalance.

Stack Confirmations

Setups form when evidence stacks: context → shift in control → clean risk. More confirmations = higher quality.

Risk First

Sizing, loss caps, and invalidations come before outcomes. Survival is the edge that compounds.

Levels ≠ Signals

Supply/Demand or S/R are areas of interest—we prepare there, then wait for our tools to confirm.

Simple, Repeatable

You’ll stop collecting strategies and start executing a consistent process you actually understand.

Feedback Loop

Journaling and weekly reviews lock in lessons fast. Progress becomes visible and measurable.

How We Trade (Simple Pipeline)

From preparation to execution—clear steps you can repeat daily.

1) Prepare

Map Market Levels (S/D, S/R, ORB) on HTF. Note news & sessions.

2) Read

Use tools: swings, BOS/CHoCH, FVG, balance. Identify a shift in control.

3) Confirm

Stack confirmations (context → signal → invalidation). If it’s clean: execute.

4) Review

Journal entries, note mistakes, adjust plan. Compound the process.
