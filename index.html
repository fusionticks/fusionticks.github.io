How We Trade

From preparation to execution, this is a clear process you can repeat daily; strategies alone are a 50/50 gamble, so we flip the odds by reading price, spotting who is in control, and stacking confirmations before any entry.

1) Prepare

Mark HTF key levels, Supply and Demand or Support and Resistance, as important buyers and sellers zones, and check the news calendar.

2) Read

Watch price when it reaches those levels and study the reaction to understand whether the market is likely to break through or reject from the zone.

3) Execute

Drop to the lower timeframe to draw new buyers and sellers zones for confirmation; if price rejects from supply for example, we want it to break the first buyers zone that formed the move before taking the entry.

4) Review

Log every trade in the journal, then review mistakes and refine the process.
