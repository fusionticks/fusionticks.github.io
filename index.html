Simple Pipeline

How We Trade

From preparation to execution—clear steps you can repeat daily.

Strategies alone are a 50/50 gamble. We flip the odds by reading the market, identifying who’s in control, and stacking confirmations before any entry.

1) Prepare

Map Market Levels (S/D, S/R, ORB) on HTF. Note news & sessions.

2) Read

Swings, BOS/CHoCH, FVG, balance. Identify a shift in control.

3) Confirm

Stack confirmations (context → signal → invalidation). If clean: execute.

4) Review

Journal entries, note mistakes, adjust plan. Compound the process.
