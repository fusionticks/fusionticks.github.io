Simple Pipeline

How We Trade

From preparation to execution, this is a clear process you can repeat daily; strategies alone are a 50/50 gamble, so we flip the odds by reading price, identifying who is in control, and stacking confirmations before any entry.

1

Prepare

Map key levels on the higher timeframe (buyers and sellers zones from S&D or S&R), then check news.

2

Read

Watch price action at your key zones and judge the reaction to spot control and bias.

3

Confirm

Drop to a lower timeframe to map tighter zones for entry and stack confirmations before executing.

4

Review

Journal every trade to review mistakes, adjust rules, and compound improvement over time.
