Course Curriculum

Three phases to go from confusion to clarity.

A. Reading the Market

  • Who’s in control? (Candlesticks)
  • Market stages (phases)
  • Price in motion (how price moves)
  • Swings & shifts
  • Breaks & flips (BOS & CHoCH)
  • Balance & imbalance (FVG)

B. Market Levels

  • Where it starts (Supply & Demand)
