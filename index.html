How We Trade

A simple, repeatable pipeline from preparation to execution.

1) Prepare

Map S/D, S/R, ORB on HTF. Note news & sessions.

2) Read

Swings, BOS/CHoCH, FVG, balance. Wait for shift in control.

3) Confirm

Stack confirmations (context → signal → invalidation). Execute only when clean.

4) Review

Journal, note mistakes, adjust plan. Compound the process.
