Simple Pipeline

How We Trade

From preparation to execution, this is a clear process you can repeat daily; strategies alone are a 50/50 gamble, so we flip the odds by reading the market, identifying who is in control, and stacking confirmations before any entry.

1

Prepare

Mark key levels on the higher timeframe: supply and demand plus support and resistance, then confirm the news.

2

Read

As price reaches those levels, watch the reaction and understand whether buyers or sellers are taking control.

3

Confirm

Drop to a lower timeframe, mark the next buyer and seller zones, and collect confirmations for entry.

4

Review

Journal every trade to spot patterns, fix mistakes, and continuously improve the process.
